## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and understand how you use our platform.

## Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function. They enable core features like authentication and security.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `session_id` | Maintains your login session | Session |
| `cookie_consent` | Stores your cookie preference | 1 year |

### Analytics Cookies

With your consent, we use Plausible Analytics to understand how visitors interact with Scivra. Plausible is privacy-focused and does not use cookies by default, but we may collect anonymized usage data.

### Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences such as language selection and experiment settings.

## Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

- **Our cookie banner**: Choose to accept or reject non-essential cookies when you first visit.
- **Browser settings**: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. See your browser's help documentation for instructions.

Note that blocking essential cookies may prevent parts of the website from functioning correctly.

## EEA/UK Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, we will only set non-essential cookies after obtaining your explicit consent, in compliance with GDPR and the ePrivacy Directive.

We may update this cookie policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
