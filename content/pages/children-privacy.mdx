## Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

Scivra is an educational physics platform. We take the privacy of children under 13 seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

## Age Restrictions

- Users under 13 years old are **not permitted** to create accounts on Scivra.
- We use an age verification gate during registration to prevent underage sign-ups.
- Anonymous access to free experiments does not require age verification.

## Information We Do Not Collect From Children

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, including:

- Names or email addresses
- Location data
- Usage tracking tied to identifiable profiles

## If We Discover Underage Data

If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will:

1. Delete the data promptly
2. Terminate the associated account
3. Notify the parent or guardian if contact information is available

## Parental Rights

Parents or guardians may:

- Request information about data collected from their child
- Request deletion of their child's data
- Refuse further collection of their child's data

To exercise these rights, contact us at [email].

## Anonymous Usage

Children may use Scivra experiments anonymously without creating an account. Anonymous usage is subject to daily time limits and does not involve collection of personally identifiable information.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page.
