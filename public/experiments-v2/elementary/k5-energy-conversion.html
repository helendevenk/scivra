Key Formulas
Conservation of Energy
\(E_{in} = E_{out} + E_{heat}\)
Efficiency
\(\eta = \frac{E_{out}}{E_{in}} \times 100\%\)
Power
\(P = \frac{E}{t} \quad (\text{Watts})\)
Heat Loss
\(E_{heat} = E_{in}(1 - \eta)\)
