Major Lunar Features

Maria — dark basaltic plains, filled ancient basins 3.5-3.9 Ga
Highlands — bright, heavily cratered ancient crust (>4 Ga)
Rilles — linear/sinuous channels; ancient lava tubes or faults
Scarps — lobate thrust faults; Moon cooling and contracting
Regolith — fine impact-generated debris blanket (1-20 m deep)
KREEP terrain — K, Rare Earth Elements, P enrichment zone

Geological Timeline

Pre-Nectarian — >3.92 Ga: accretion, magma ocean, LHB
Nectarian — 3.92-3.85 Ga: heavy bombardment ends
Imbrian — 3.85-3.2 Ga: mare volcanism (dark lava plains)
Eratosthenian — 3.2-1.1 Ga: declining volcanism
Copernican — 1.1 Ga-present: impact gardening only
