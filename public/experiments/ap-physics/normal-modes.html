About Normal Modes

A standing wave forms when two identical waves travel in opposite directions and interfere. The nth normal mode of a string fixed at both ends has n half-wavelengths fitting in the string length L. Nodes (zero displacement) are marked red; antinodes (maximum displacement) are marked blue.
