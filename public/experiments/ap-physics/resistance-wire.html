About This Experiment

Explore how wire resistance depends on resistivity (ρ), length (L), and cross-sectional area (A).

The 3D wire dynamically changes shape. Internal electrons show how easily current flows — higher resistance means slower, fewer visible electrons.

The cross-section view on the right shows the wire's circular area. Try different materials and dimensions to see how R changes!
