About Fourier Synthesis

Any periodic waveform can be decomposed into (or built from) a sum of sinusoidal harmonics. Adjust the amplitude sliders to see how individual sine waves combine. Try the presets to see classic waveform constructions.

The 3D surface shows the composite wave propagating through space. Drag to orbit.
