How to Use

1. Select a preset or choose "Custom Draw" to sketch your own function.
2. Hover over f(x) graph to see the tangent line and slope.
3. The derivative and integral are computed automatically.
4. Adjust amplitude, frequency, and range with sliders.

Calculus Grapher

Visualize the fundamental relationship between a function, its derivative, and its integral. The derivative shows the rate of change (slope) at each point. The integral shows the accumulated area under the curve.
