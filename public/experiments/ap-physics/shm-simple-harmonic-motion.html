CONCEPT
SHM occurs when a restoring force is proportional to displacement: F = -kx. This is Hooke's Law. The negative sign means force always points back toward equilibrium — the defining feature of oscillation.

Energy oscillates between kinetic (fast at center) and potential (stored in spring at extremes). In an ideal system these sum to a constant. Real damping drains energy as heat.

The phase-space ellipse (x vs v) is a beautiful signature: undamped = perfect ellipse; damped = inward spiral toward origin.
DID YOU KNOW
⏱ Galileo discovered isochronism by timing his own pulse against a chandelier swinging in Pisa Cathedral (~1583).
⚠️ Common misconception: heavier mass does NOT make the spring oscillate faster — it actually slows it down (T ∝ √m).
