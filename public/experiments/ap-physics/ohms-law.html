How It Works

Ohm's Law states that current through a conductor is directly proportional to voltage and inversely proportional to resistance.

Watch the electrons (blue dots) flow through the circuit. Their speed is proportional to current. The bulb brightness and resistor color show power dissipation.

Drag to orbit. Scroll to zoom. Try the presets to see different regimes.
