How to Explore

The 3D scene shows the Bohr model with orbits n=1 to 6. The electron orbits at the current level n. Use the sliders to change energy level or select a transition (n_i → n_f) and click "Fire Transition" to watch the electron jump and emit a photon. The emitted photon color matches its wavelength. The energy diagram and spectrum bar update in real time. Orbit with mouse drag, zoom with scroll.
