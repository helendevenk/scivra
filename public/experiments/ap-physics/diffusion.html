Quick Quiz
1. If the temperature is doubled, the average molecular speed:
A) Doubles
B) Increases by a factor of √2
C) Quadruples
D) Stays the same
Correct! The RMS speed v ∝ √T, so doubling T increases speed by √2 ≈ 1.41×.
2. In Fick's Law J = −D(dC/dx), a steeper concentration gradient means:
A) Greater diffusion flux
B) Smaller diffusion flux
C) No effect on flux
D) Diffusion stops
Correct! A larger concentration gradient (dC/dx) produces a larger magnitude of flux J. Diffusion naturally moves particles from high to low concentration.
