Concept

A gravitational field is a region of space where any mass experiences a force. The field g points toward the source mass and grows stronger as you approach it — an inverse-square relationship.

The gravitational potential well (bowl shape) shows that you must do work to climb out. Deeper in the well = more tightly bound. A black hole is an infinitely deep well that nothing can escape.

Escape velocity is the speed at which KE exactly cancels the (negative) gravitational PE. At that threshold, total mechanical energy is exactly zero.

Knowledge Cards

Historical Fact

Newton derived the inverse-square law by noting that the Moon's centripetal acceleration is ~1/3600 of surface g — exactly (60 R_E)² ratio.

Common Misconception

Gravity in orbit is NOT zero. Astronauts are weightless because they are in free-fall, not because gravity vanished. g at ISS altitude is ~88% of surface value.

Advanced Connection

In General Relativity, the potential well is literal spacetime curvature. Gravitational time dilation is directly proportional to the depth in the well.
