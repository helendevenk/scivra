Conceptual Insight
Why CO2 causes warming
CO2 absorbs infrared radiation emitted by Earth's surface. The molecule's vibrational modes match IR frequencies, trapping heat that would otherwise escape to space.
Why the sky doesn't block visible light
N2 and O2 (99% of atmosphere) have no vibrational absorption in the visible range. Their bond symmetry means visible photons pass through without interaction.
