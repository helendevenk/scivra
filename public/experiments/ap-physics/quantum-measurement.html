Key Insight
Measurement fundamentally changes a quantum system. Before measurement, a particle exists in a superposition of positions described by |psi(x)|^2. The act of measurement "collapses" the wave function to a definite position. Repeated measurements on identical systems build up a distribution matching |psi|^2.
