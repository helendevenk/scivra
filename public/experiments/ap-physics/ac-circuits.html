How to Read This

Glowing electrons alternate direction with the AC source. The waveform shows voltage (blue) and current (yellow) with their phase difference. The phasor diagram rotates in real-time. The impedance triangle shows R, X_L-X_C, and Z relationships. Try the RLC Resonance preset to see maximum current at f0.
