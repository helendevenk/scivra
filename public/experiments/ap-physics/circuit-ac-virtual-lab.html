AC Virtual Lab

Build and explore series RLC circuits with alternating current. Electrons oscillate back and forth as the AC source reverses polarity. Watch the waveform to see V(t) and I(t) phase relationship. The rotating phasor diagram shows voltage and current vectors in real time.

Try adjusting frequency toward the resonant frequency (shown in gold) to see maximum current and power factor = 1.
