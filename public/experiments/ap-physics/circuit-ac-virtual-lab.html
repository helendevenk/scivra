Concept
In a series RLC circuit, resistors dissipate energy while inductors and capacitors store it — and their effects oppose each other. At resonance, XL = XC, so they cancel and impedance drops to just R.
Phase angle φ determines whether the circuit is inductive (φ>0, current lags) or capacitive (φ<0, current leads). Maximum power transfer occurs at φ = 0.
Knowledge Cards
⚡ Tesla's Radio Patent — Resonant LC circuits are the core of every radio tuner. Q factor determines selectivity.
🎯 Common Misconception — At resonance, Z ≠ 0. Minimum Z = R. Energy still dissipates in resistance.
📡 AP Connection — AP Physics C: E&M Unit 4. Phasor diagrams and impedance triangle are exam favorites.
Why It Matters
• Radio/TV tuners select frequencies via LC resonance
• Power factor correction in industrial AC systems
• MRI machines use tuned RLC coils at precise resonance
