Six Simple Machines Mechanical Advantage · K5 Science
Key Formula
Mechanical Advantage: output force divided by input force. Greater than 1 means the machine helps you!
Work stays the same — simple machines just let you use less force over a greater distance.
Pick a Machine
A lever has a fulcrum (pivot point). Push one side down and the other side goes up! Like a see-saw.
Fun Fact
Archimedes said "Give me a lever long enough and I will move the Earth!" He understood mechanical advantage over 2,000 years ago.
Remember
Simple machines don't create energy — they change HOW you apply force. You always do the same total work!
Why It Matters
Simple machines are everywhere: scissors (wedge), doorknobs (wheel & axle), ramps (inclined plane), and bottle caps (screw)!
