Photosynthesis Formula

What Plants Need

☀️ Sunlight — Energy source for making food through photosynthesis. More light = faster growth.
💧 Water — Carries nutrients from soil, cools plant, needed for photosynthesis.
🌬️ CO₂ — Carbon dioxide from the air combines with water to make glucose.
🌍 Nutrients — Minerals from soil (nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium) for healthy growth.
🌡️ Temperature — Most plants grow best between 15–30°C. Too hot or cold slows growth.

Products of Photosynthesis

Plants produce glucose (food for energy and growth) and release oxygen as a byproduct — the oxygen we breathe!

Why It Matters

Plants are producers — the foundation of almost every food chain on Earth. They convert sunlight energy into chemical energy that all other organisms depend on.
