Key Formulas
Kinetic Energy of a Particle
\(E_k = \tfrac{1}{2}mv^2\)
Average Kinetic Energy & Temperature
\(\bar{E}_k = \tfrac{3}{2}k_B T\)
Phase Transitions (H₂O at 1 atm)
\(T_{\text{melt}}=273\,\text{K},\quad T_{\text{boil}}=373\,\text{K}\)
Ideal Gas Law
\(PV = nRT\)
Real-World Applications
Ice in a drink absorbs heat and melts (solid → liquid)
Steam turbines convert gas expansion into electricity
Refrigerators cycle refrigerant between gas and liquid
