Key Formulas

Weather Types

☀️ Clear Sky — High pressure system, low humidity, light winds. Warm and sunny.
⛈️ Thunderstorm — Warm moist air rises rapidly, forms cumulonimbus clouds, lightning and heavy rain.
🌨️ Blizzard — Cold air mass, high winds (>56 km/h), heavy snow, low visibility.

Weather vs. Climate

Weather is the short-term state of the atmosphere — what it's doing right now or this week.

Climate is the long-term average weather pattern in a region over 30+ years.

Weather Fronts

A cold front is when cold air pushes under warm air — brings storms. A warm front is when warm air slides over cold air — brings gentle rain.

Why It Matters

Weather affects agriculture, transportation, energy use, and human health. Weather forecasting saves thousands of lives annually by predicting dangerous storms.
