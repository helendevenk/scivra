Key Formulas
Erosion Rate (simplified)
\(E = k \cdot v^2 \cdot t\)
Sediment Transport (Hjulström)
\(v_c \propto \sqrt{d}\)
Total Material Removed
\(\Delta V = E \cdot A \cdot t\)
Slope Angle Effect
\(E_s = E_0 \cdot \sin(\theta)\)
Real-World Examples

Erosion Agents
Water — Carries sediment, cuts valleys, forms deltas
Wind — Moves sand particles, shapes dunes
Ice — Glaciers carve U-shaped valleys and moraines
Current Landform Info
A river canyon forms when flowing water cuts downward through rock layers. The V-shaped cross section and steep walls are characteristic features.
