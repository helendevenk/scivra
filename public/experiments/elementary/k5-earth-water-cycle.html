Key Formulas

Evaporation Energy: $Q = m \times L_v$

where $m$ is mass, $L_v$ is latent heat of vaporization (2260 kJ/kg)

Water Balance: $P = E + R + \Delta S$

Precipitation = Evaporation + Runoff + Storage change

☀️ Evaporation

The sun heats water in oceans, lakes, and rivers. Water changes from liquid to water vapor (gas) and rises into the air.

☁️ Condensation

Water vapor cools as it rises and changes back into tiny water droplets, forming clouds. This is why clouds float in the sky!

🌧️ Precipitation

When water droplets in clouds get too heavy, they fall as rain, snow, sleet, or hail. This returns water to Earth's surface.

🏔️ Collection

Water collects in oceans, lakes, rivers, and underground. Some soaks into the ground (infiltration) and some flows over land (runoff).

🔄 Continuous Cycle

The water cycle never stops! The same water has been cycling through Earth for billions of years. Water you drink today might have been in a dinosaur!
