Energy Conversion PE → KE → Heat · K5 Science
Key Formulas
Potential energy: the higher the object, the more stored energy it has (like a ball at the top of a hill).
Kinetic energy: moving objects have energy. Faster and heavier = more kinetic energy!
What's Happening?

Energy can change from one form to another, but it's never created or destroyed — this is the Law of Conservation of Energy!

Watch a ball drop: its Potential Energy (PE) at the top converts to Kinetic Energy (KE) as it falls. Some turns into heat from air resistance.

Energy forms: ⚡ electrical, 💡 light, 🔊 sound, 🌡️ thermal, 🏃 kinetic, 🌿 chemical.

Remember
Energy is never lost — it just changes form. Even "wasted" energy (like heat) still exists in the universe!
Why It Matters
Power plants convert chemical/nuclear energy to electricity. Your body converts food (chemical energy) to movement and heat every day!
