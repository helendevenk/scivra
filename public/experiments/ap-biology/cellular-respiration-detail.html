Protein Complexes
INADH Dehydrogenase — oxidizes NADH → NAD⁺, pumps 4 H⁺ to intermembrane space. Inhibited by rotenone.
IISuccinate Dehydrogenase — oxidizes FADH₂ → FAD, does NOT pump H⁺. Embedded in membrane.
IIICytochrome bc1 — transfers electrons via Q cycle, pumps 4 H⁺. Inhibited by antimycin A.
IVCytochrome c Oxidase — final electron acceptor, reduces O₂ → H₂O, pumps 2 H⁺. Inhibited by cyanide, CO.
VATP Synthase (F₁F₀) — protons flow back driving rotation of c-ring → γ-subunit rotation → ATP synthesis.

Why It Matters — AP Bio
Big Idea 2. AP FRQ: explain how cyanide poisoning kills cells (blocks Complex IV, halts O₂ reduction, collapses gradient, ATP synthesis stops). DNP is an uncoupler — dissipates proton gradient as heat without ATP production (historical "diet pill"). Oligomycin blocks ATP synthase pore directly.
