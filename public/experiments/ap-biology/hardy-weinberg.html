HW Conditions (5 Assumptions)
✅Large population — no genetic drift
✅No mutation — allele frequencies stable
✅No migration — closed gene pool
✅No natural selection — all genotypes equally fit
✅Random mating — panmixia
Why It Matters — AP Bio
Big Idea 1 (Evolution). AP FRQ: use HW to calculate allele frequencies from observed genotype counts, determine if a population is evolving, identify which assumption is violated. Understand that HW is a null hypothesis — deviations indicate evolutionary forces at work.
